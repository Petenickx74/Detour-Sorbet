Our Story

Born from a generational love of dessert

Our story begins in 2010, when Sam started making small-batch sorbet for farmers markets in the SF Bay Area — experimenting with over 55 unique flavors and falling deeper in love with the craft of delicious deserts. The pursuit of using only the best ingredients and bold flavor never left him.

The passion for frozen dessert runs in the family. Sam's grandfather, Papi Booth, was a lifelong devotee of the great ice cream makers — a passion that was passed down and transformed. In 2024, that generational love found its truest expression: Detour Sorbet, born from decades of curiosity, craft, and a belief that the best things are made simply, and shared freely.

Craft & Care

How we make it

Every batch follows the same four steps — obsessively, every single time.

🌱
Step 01
Source

We partner directly with regional farms, selecting only fruit at peak ripeness — never frozen or imported off-season.

⚖️
Step 02
Measure

Each recipe is precise to the gram. Pure cane sugar, filtered water, and fruit — nothing else makes the list.

🌀
Step 03
Churn

Slow-churned in small batches for the perfect texture — smooth, dense, and packed with fruit flavor.

📦
Step 04
Pack & Deliver

Pulled fresh and packed to order. Delivered cold within 24 hours, or find us at your local market.
